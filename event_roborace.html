Robo Race

A High-Speed Innovation Challenge

Welcome to Robo Race

Robo Race is an electrifying technical event designed to challenge the speed, agility, and innovation of robotic designs.

Rules

Participants must design and build their own robots to race against competitors on a predefined track. Teams will be judged based on speed, design innovation, obstacle-handling capability, and overall performance.

Judging Criteria

Participants will be evaluated based on the following key aspects: The time taken to complete the track will be a major factor.
