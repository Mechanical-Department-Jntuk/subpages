Welcome to Bridge Battleground

Are you ready to put your engineering skills to the test and build a structure that stands strong against the forces of nature? Bridge Battleground is an exciting challenge where participants must design and construct a sturdy bridge using limited materials while demonstrating their knowledge of structural mechanics and load distribution. This event combines creativity, technical expertise, and hands-on problem-solving.

Event Highlights

Materials Provided: Participants will be given a set of materials (e.g., popsicle sticks, straws, glue, threads) and must construct a strong and stable bridge within the given time.

Time Limit: Participants will have 2 hours to complete their bridge.

Testing Criteria: The bridge will be tested for strength, load-bearing capacity, and efficiency.

Prizes: Exciting prizes await those who can combine smart engineering with innovative thinking.

Judging Criteria

Strength: The bridge must withstand the maximum load without collapsing.

Efficiency: The bridge should use materials optimally with minimal waste.

Design: Creativity and innovation in the bridge's design will be evaluated.

Adherence to Rules: Compliance with the provided guidelines and time limit.
