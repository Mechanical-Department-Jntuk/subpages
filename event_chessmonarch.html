Tournament Details

Mode: Online
Platform: Lichess
Format: Swiss tournament
Rounds: Seven rounds
Time Control: 15 minutes (No increment)
Date: 14-05-2025
Entry Fee: Rs 100/-

Rules and Regulations

  • All participants must have a Lichess account and be familiar with the platform's rules and regulations.
  • The tournament will be played with a standard chess clock, with no time increment per move.
  • Players are expected to be online and ready to play at the scheduled start time for each round.
  • Any player who is not online and ready to play within 10 minutes of the scheduled start time will be forfeited.
  • The tournament director's decisions are final and binding.

Prizes and Recognition

Winner: The winner of the tournament will be crowned the Chess Monarch and will receive a prize.

Top 3 Players: The top 3 players will receive prizes and certificates of recognition.

All Participants: All participants will receive a certificate of participation and a chance to improve their chess skills.

How to Participate

  1. Create a Lichess account if you don't already have one.
  2. Register for the tournament by clicking on the "Register Now" button above or scanning the QR code on the official Torque poster.
  3. Pay the entry fee of Rs 100/- through the provided payment methods.
  4. Make sure to be online and ready to play at the scheduled start time for each round.
