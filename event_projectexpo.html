Welcome to Project Expo

A Celebration of Creativity, Innovation, and Groundbreaking Ideas! Join us on a journey to unleash the power of imagination and innovation. Project Exhibition: Teams (of maximum 4) will showcase their projects, prototypes, and posters during the event.
